Why invest in BananaCoin

Bananacoin is the world's first blockchain option for investing in the production of organic bananas in Vientiane province, the Lao PDR.
  1. The deficit of export of bananas to China is estimated at 30,000 hectares, the consumption growth is increasing and more Chinese plantations are being closed for the use of chemistry
  2. The price of token Bananacoin is protected by the cost of one kilogram of bananas
  3. Having invested in Bananacoin you have the opportunity to get a minimum of 100% profit of the project implementation
  4. Our business is completely transparent, we have already passed all stages of its development and consider ourselves to be professionals in it
  5. Bananacoin is a full-fledged financial instrument that will be freely traded on the exchange
  6. Every investor has the right to personally visit our plantations in Vientiane province, the Lao PDR
  7. Our business is easy to scale and it is growing into a serious player in the Asian fruit market
  8. Cultivated variety of bananas Finger Lady is the most expensive and the most popular on the Chinese market
