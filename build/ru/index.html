    Зачем инвестировать в Banancoin

    Bananacoin is the world’s first blockchain option for investing in the production of organic bananas in Vientiane province, the Lao PDR.

    1. The deficit of export of bananas to China is estimated at 30,000 hectares, the consumption growth is increasing
    2. The price of token Bananacoin is protected by the cost of one kilogram of bananas
    3. Bananacoin is a full-fledged financial instrument that will be freely traded on the exchange
    4. Having invested in Bananacoin you have the opportunity to get a minimum of 100% profit of the project implementation
    5. Every investor has the right to personally visit our plantations in Vientiane province, the Lao PDR
    6. Our business is easy to scale and it is growing into a serious player in the Asian fruit market

    Some frequently asked questions

    We advocate full transparency, so if you don’t see your questions answered in our FAQ, please write us and we’ll get back to you.

    Что такое TGE?
    TGE (Первичная генерация токенов) - это модель краудфандинга, которая позволяет нам выпускать цифровые токены в обмен на инвестиции. Каждый токен дает право его владельцу на получение прибыли по объявленным условиям.
    Who you are?
    We are owners of the real banana plantation, you can get acquainted with infotmation about our team and developers on the main page of the site https://bananacoin.io/, and also chat personally at the https://t.me/bananacoineng chat telegram.

    How can agro-industry be tied to a blockchain ?
    We do not tie the agro-industry to the blockchain, we use the capabilities of a blockchain to raise investment (crowdfunding) and arrange transparent payments to investors in the future.
    Do you have a Bounty program?
    Yes, the incentive program has already started and will operate until the end of ICO. https://bitcointalk.org/index.php?topic=2164386.0 - detailed conditions and registration forms by reference.
    Why Laos, why not Vietnam or not China?
    Right now we are already engaged in farming in Laos, we have permits from local goverment and wide tax preferences. To conduct such business is much more expensive in China, Vietnam also refers to ecologically unfavorable territories on the agricultural sector (chemical pollution after the war).
